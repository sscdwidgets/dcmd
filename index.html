Settings

Profitability Lens

Projected COF = Current COF + Beta × ΔYield. Spread = market rate − benchmark. Color coding scores each rate by its spread vs the product's benchmark.

Grading thresholds

Rates at or below the green threshold show green, between green and orange show orange, and above orange show red.

Pass-through timing

Holds your projected COF flat for the first N months before it starts responding to yield curve moves — reflecting the real-world delay between Fed rate changes and your portfolio repricing. Typical community-bank lag is 6–9 months.

CD benchmarks
.

CDs are expected to cost more than blended COF, so each CD family compares to COF + premium instead of COF directly. Override mode swaps that for matched-tenor yield curve + spread, useful when evaluating CDs as a replacement for wholesale funding (FHLB advances, brokered CDs).
